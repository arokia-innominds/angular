Functional safety has become a critically important issue across all areas of industry with interactions and dependencies between functions and subsystems – this is especially crucial in complex and distributed systems.

With the expanding digitalization and automation of life and industry also new challenges and requirements for functional safety technology arise. Based on your existing architecture, we identify threats, assess pitfalls, derive safety objectives, and develop safety concepts.

Our experience in safety management and the development of safety-critical functions has been proven across a variety of industries, from concept to validation.
